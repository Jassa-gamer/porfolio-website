Frequently Asked Questions

How Long it takes to make my website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A simple brochure website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex e-commerce sites may take 4-6 weeks. After our initial consultation, I'll provide you with a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

What platforms do you work with?

I primarily work with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript. For CMS solutions, I specialize in Figma (industry level software), webdflow, framer, wordpress. I choose the platform that best fits your specific needs and goals.

Do you provide hosting services?

While I don't provide hosting directly, I can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your needs. I also offer assistance with setting up your hosting account and can manage the technical aspects of getting your site live.

Can you help with website maintenance?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages that include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and content updates. These can be customized to your specific needs and budget.

What's included in your pricing?

My pricing includes custom design, development, responsive implementation, basic SEO setup, and initial training on how to manage your content. Additional services like custom functionality, e-commerce setup, or advanced SEO can be added as needed. All projects include a 30-day warranty period for any bug fixes.
