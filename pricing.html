Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in these prices?

All prices include design, development, and basic setup. You'll receive a fully functional website ready to launch. Hosting and domain registration are not included but I can help you set them up.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes! After your website is complete, I offer maintenance packages starting at just $49/month for updates, security checks, and minor content changes.

How long does it take to complete a website?

Basic websites typically take 1 week, standard sites 2 weeks, and e-commerce sites 3 weeks. These timelines may vary based on project complexity and your response time for feedback.

What if I need more pages than included?

Additional pages can be added at $50 per page. We can discuss your specific needs and adjust the package accordingly.

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

I'm committed to your satisfaction. If you're not happy with the initial design, I'll work with you to make it right. No extra charges for revisions within the agreed scope.
